# Business Associate Agreement (BAA) — Template (HIPAA)

**Last updated:** April 2026  

**Important:** This is a **template** for discussion with **U.S. HIPAA**-regulated customers only. **Do not** use as a substitute for **legal advice**. Execute only when Customer is a **covered entity** or **business associate** and will submit **PHI** to MineAid HMS, as referenced in the **Commercial Agreement** section 8.

---

This Business Associate Agreement (“**BAA**”) is entered into as of **[Effective Date]** by and between:

**Covered Entity / Business Associate:** **[Customer legal name]** (“**CE**”)  

**Business Associate:** **Nuestman Tech Solutions** (“**BA**”)  

CE and BA agree as follows:

## 1. Definitions

Terms **Business Associate**, **Covered Entity**, **HIPAA**, **Protected Health Information (PHI)**, **HITECH**, and **Subcontractor** have the meanings in **45 CFR Parts 160 and 164**.

## 2. Permitted uses and disclosures

2.1 BA may use and disclose PHI only to **provide** the services described in the **Commercial Agreement** (MineAid HMS) and as permitted by this BAA, the **underlying agreement**, or as **required by law**.

2.2 BA may use PHI for **proper management and administration** of BA and to **carry out legal responsibilities** of BA, if disclosures are **required by law** or BA obtains **reasonable assurances** from the recipient that PHI will be held confidentially and used or further disclosed only as required by law or for the purpose for which it was disclosed.

## 3. Safeguards

BA will implement **appropriate safeguards** to prevent use or disclosure of PHI other than as permitted by this BAA and will comply with **Subpart C of 45 CFR Part 164** with respect to electronic PHI.

## 4. Subcontractors

BA will ensure that any **subcontractor** that creates, receives, maintains, or transmits PHI on behalf of BA agrees in writing to **substantially the same** restrictions and conditions that apply to BA under this BAA.

## 5. Access and amendments

BA will make PHI in a **designated record set** available to CE as required for CE to meet **45 CFR 164.524** and will make PHI available for **amendment** and incorporate amendments as required by **45 CFR 164.526**, in each case per the **functionality** of the Service and reasonable cooperation.

## 6. Accounting and HHS

6.1 BA will document disclosures as required for CE to make available an **accounting** in accordance with **45 CFR 164.528**.

6.2 BA will make internal practices, books, and records relating to use and disclosure of PHI available to **HHS** for purposes of determining CE’s or BA’s compliance with HIPAA.

## 7. Breach notification

BA will notify CE **without unreasonable delay** of any **breach** of unsecured PHI, in accordance with **45 CFR 164.410**.

## 8. Minimum necessary; de-identification

BA will only request, use, or disclose the **minimum necessary** PHI unless the minimum necessary standard does not apply. BA may **de-identify** PHI in accordance with **45 CFR 164.514** when agreed in writing.

## 9. Term and termination

9.1 This BAA terminates when the **underlying agreement** terminates or when BA is no longer a business associate of CE, or when BA or CE terminates for **breach** as permitted by **45 CFR 164.504(e)(2)(iii)**.

9.2 Upon termination, BA will return or **destroy** PHI received from CE (or created or received on CE’s behalf) if feasible; if infeasible, BA will extend protections and limit use to those purposes that make return or destruction infeasible.

## 10. Miscellaneous

10.1 This BAA supplements the **Commercial Agreement** and **Terms**. In case of conflict on **PHI**, this BAA controls.

10.2 **Governing law.** As in the Commercial Agreement, without waiving HIPAA federal preemption where applicable.
